Individual Chocolate Lava Cakes

The chocolate molten lava cakes are delicious and will melt across your plate.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 60% cacao Ghirardelli bittersweet baking chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cake flour

Instructions

  1. To make centers, melt 2 ounces of chocolate (1/2 a baking bar) and cream in double boiler. Whisk gently to blend.
  2. Refrigerate about 2 hours or until firm.
  3. Form into 6 balls; refrigerate until needed.
  4. Heat oven to 400Â°F.
  5. Spray six 4-ounce ramekins or custard cups with cooking spray
  6. Melt 4 ounces of chocolate (1 baking bar) and butter in double boiler; whisk gently to blend.
  7. With an electric mixer, whisk eggs, yolks, sugar, and vanilla on high speed about 5 minutes or until thick and light.
  8. Fold melted chocolate mixture and flour into egg mixture just until combined.
  9. Spoon cake batter into ramekins. Place a chocolate ball in the middle of each ramekin.
  10. Bake about 15 minutes or until cake is firm to the touch. Let it sit out of the oven for about 5 minutes.
  11. Run a small, sharp knife around the inside of each ramekin, plate on top, invert, and remove ramekin. Garnish with raspberries and vanilla ice cream.
